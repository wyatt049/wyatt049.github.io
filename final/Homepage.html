D&D
D&D is a tabletop role-playing game where players create characters and embark on adventures in a fantasy world.

The game is typically led by a Dungeon Master or as most people call them The "DM" who narrates the story and controls the game world, while players make decisions as their characters and while using diffrent dice to see how well they do with particullar actions.

Today we will be going over the basics of how to play D&D things like charcter creation and world building for your Lovely DM.
