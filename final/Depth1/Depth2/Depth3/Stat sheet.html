What Is a Stat Sheet?

A Stat Sheet is the mechanical summary of a character. It shows their core abilities, strengths, weaknesses, and the numbers that define how they interact with the world. While the backstory explains who the character is, the stat sheet explains what they can do. The image below is a partial stat sheet because they are usually 2-3 pages long.

Below are the major components that make up a stat sheet. Each section has its own page elsewhere on the site where you can learn more about how it works.

Backstory

A brief summary of the character’s history and motivations. Backstory

Universal Features

Basic traits every character has, such as HP, AC, movement, and more. Universal Features

Classes

Determines the character’s role and abilities. Classes

Skills

Represents trained or natural talents. Skills

Stats

Core ability scores that define the character’s strengths. Stats
