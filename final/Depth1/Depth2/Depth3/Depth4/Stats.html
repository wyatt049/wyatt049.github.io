Stats

What Are Stats?

Stats, also known as ability scores, represent a character’s natural strengths and weaknesses. They influence almost everything a character does, from attacking and defending to solving problems and interacting with the world.

Each stat has a score (usually between 1 and 20) which determines the modifier, which is the number added to rolls that use that stat. above 11 means a positive modifier. While below 10 means a negative modifier.

Strength: Physical power, lifting, carrying, and melee attacks.

Dexterity: Agility, balance, reflexes, stealth, and ranged attacks.

Constitution: Endurance, toughness, resisting poison, and determining hit points.

Intelligence: Logic, memory, knowledge skills, and understanding complex ideas.

Wisdom: Awareness, perception, intuition, and resisting mental effects.

Charisma: Presence, confidence, persuasion, deception, and social influence.
