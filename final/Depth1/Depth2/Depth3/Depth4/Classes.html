Classes

What Are Classes?

A class defines what a character does and how they approach challenges. It determines their abilities, combat style, special features, and how they grow stronger as they level up. Classes are the biggest influence on a character’s role in the game, shaping everything from how they fight to how they solve problems.

Each class below includes a simple explanation of what makes it unique. This page gives you a clear overview without diving into deep rules or advanced mechanics.

Barbarian

Fierce warriors who rely on raw strength and primal fury. Barbarians excel in close combat and can withstand heavy damage thanks to their rage and toughness.

Bard

Magical performers who inspire allies and manipulate the battlefield with music, charm, and versatile spellcasting. Bards are adaptable and creative problem‑solvers.

Cleric

Divine spellcasters who draw power from a deity. Clerics can heal, protect, and smite enemies, making them one of the most balanced and dependable classes.

Druid

Nature‑based spellcasters who can shapeshift into animals and command the forces of the natural world. Druids are flexible and powerful in both magic and utility.

Fighter

Masters of weapons and combat techniques. Fighters are reliable, adaptable, and effective in almost any battle situation, with many ways to specialize.

Monk

Martial artists who use speed, discipline, and spiritual energy to strike quickly and avoid harm. Monks excel at mobility and precision.

Paladin

Holy warriors bound by an oath. Paladins combine martial skill with divine magic, protecting allies and smiting evil with righteous power.

Ranger

Skilled hunters and trackers who blend martial ability with nature magic. Rangers excel at exploration, ranged combat, and fighting specific types of enemies.

Rogue

Stealthy, agile characters who rely on precision and cunning. Rogues are experts at sneaking, lockpicking, and dealing high damage with sneak attacks.

Sorcerer

Spellcasters who wield magic through innate power rather than study. Sorcerers have fewer spells but can shape and enhance them in unique ways.

Warlock

Magic users who gain power through a pact with a powerful being. Warlocks specialize in eldritch magic and have a mix of spells and supernatural abilities.

Wizard

Scholars of arcane magic who learn spells through study and practice. Wizards have the largest spell selection and excel at controlling the battlefield.
