Dungeon Master

The Dungeon Master (DM) is the person who runs the game world. They describe what the players see, control the creatures they meet, and guide the story as it unfolds. The DM isn’t against the players — their job is to challenge them, react to their choices, and help everyone have fun.

Basic Responsibilities

  • Describe the World — Explain what the players see, hear, and experience.
  • Run Encounters — Control monsters, NPCs, and anything the players interact with.
  • Adjudicate Rules — Make fair calls when something unexpected happens.
  • Guide the Story — Keep the adventure moving and respond to player decisions.
  • Prepare Content — Create notes, maps, and ideas for future sessions.
